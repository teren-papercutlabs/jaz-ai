### Security Accreditations
Source: https://help.jaz.ai/en/articles/12837847-security-accreditations

**Q1. What accreditations does Jaz have?**

- Jaz is **ISO 27001** and**SOC 2** certified. These certifications show our commitment to protecting your data and maintaining strong security practices.

**Q2. What is ISO 27001?**

- ISO 27001 is a global standard for information security. It confirms that Jaz has strict internal processes in place to manage and protect data.

**Q3. What is SOC 2?**

- SOC 2 is an audit report that evaluates how a company protects customer data based on the Trust Service Criteria (security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, privacy).

**Q4. Why do these certifications matter?**

- These certifications help ensure that your business data is handled securely at all times.

**Q5. How often are these certifications maintained?**

- Both ISO 27001 and SOC 2 require continuous compliance. Jaz undergoes regular audits to make sure our processes, systems, and controls stay aligned with certification requirements.
