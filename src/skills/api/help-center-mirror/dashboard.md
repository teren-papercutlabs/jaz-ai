### Jaz Dashboard
Source: https://help.jaz.ai/en/articles/9095833-jaz-dashboard

**Q1. Are cross-currency transactions included in the Account Receivable/Payable amounts on the dashboard?**

- Yes, cross-currency transactions are included in the account receivables / payable amounts.
- The cross-currency transaction amounts are converted to base currency amounts using the exchange rate of the transaction and then added together with the rest of your transactions made in base currency.

**Q2. How do I read the cash flow graph in Jaz?**

- The top portion of the graph indicates cash In-flow in your organization for the selected period.
- The bottom portion of the graph indicates cash out-flow in your organization for the selected period.
- The white dot in each bar signifies net cashflow for the selected period.
- The white line illustrates a trend of net cashflow in your organization for the selected period.
- You can adjust the period using the calendar.

**Q3. How do I adjust the dates to see different periods on the cash flow graph in Jaz?**

- You can change the date range using the calendar. The graph will be updated accordingly.

**Q4. What is the significance of the Chart of Accounts in the dashboard?**

- Accounts shown in the dashboard under **Chart of Accounts**are accounts with the top 5 monthly movements.

**Q5. Can I see payment accounts in the Chart of Account card on the dashboard?**

- No, only the top 5 non-payment accounts will be shown on the COA card on the dashboard.
